Customising Big Cartel - Part 2

The next step to customising the "Sexy" theme is to move the menu from the right. Most requests I have seen are to have the menu placed above the main content.

The first step is to find the menu contents.

This is the whole of the right side bar, and has quite a few customisable options. For my project, I only want one part - the bit that contains the link to all your pages, so delete the rest of the code. You can still work with it later.

The below code outputs a list of links to all your pages.


<div id="navigation">
      <div id="help" class="pod">
        <h3 class="help-title"><span>Info</span></h3>
        <ul class="help-list">
          {% for page in pages.all %}<li>{{ page | link_to }}</li>{% endfor %}<li>{{ pages.contact | link_to }}</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
</div>

This will however miss out your products, and a link to your home page. I am going to assume that you will be using a separate "Home" and "Products" page.


<div id="navigation">
      
        
        <ul class="help-list">
          <li><a href="/">Home</a></li>
          <li><a href="/products">Products</a></li>
          {% for page in pages.all %}<li>{{ page | link_to }}</li>{% endfor %}<li>{{ pages.contact | link_to }}</li>
        </ul>
      
</div>

By adding those in, you allow your user to navigate back to the home page, and to view a list of all your products. Place this code below the div with an id of "header".
