Running

I love running! It's been a huge part of my social life since I moved to London in 2013, as well as my main source of fitness and mental resilience. There's nothing better than starting the day with a rin on a nice spring morning, honest!

I run a lot. Since moving to London, I've run with Run Dem Crew, LDN Brunch Club, and now with Track East, where I'm one of the captains.

My social life revoles around running, and I've met some really awesome people through the various communities I've run with. More writing to come about those people.

I set myself a challenge to complete 100 races before I turned 30. I managed it!
