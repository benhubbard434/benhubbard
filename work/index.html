Offcuts by Office

#shopify

#2015

Office wanted an online hub to distribute exclusive last pairs of shoes. We worked together within their branding guidelines to build a online store using Shopify.

Clare Payne

#portfolio

#shopify

#2015

Clare asked be to build her a portfolio site for her collections, which doubles up as a shop to sell limited pieces from her pieces.

Working closely with Clare, we tossed around some ideas to create a site which allowed the work to stand front and centre. The biggest challenge of the build was translating the structure of her collections into a navigation that made sense, as they grew season on season.
